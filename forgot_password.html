TapInSolutions (TiS) is a software solutions service for businesses wanting to build customer generation and retention, compile data and engage customers to promote, authenticate and track their products and services. TiS’s customized Near-Field-Communication (NFC) and Text-to-Land (T2L) software solutions will help businesses instantly connect and engage consumers with their brands and services right on the client’s own smartphone, without the client ever having to go to the trouble of configuring a connection.
